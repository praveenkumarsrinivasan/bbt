			<div class="entrytext">
				<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: On the deck of a ship on the North Sea, in the middle of a storm. Leonard is on a sat-phone.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Sheldon, it&#8217;s not a great time, what do you want?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon <i>(in the apartment)</i>: Hello to you, too. I&#8217;m sorry, but this is important.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: What is it?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Back to the Future II was in the Back to the Future III case, and Back to the Future III was, get this, in the Back to the Future II case.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: So?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: So, did you do that, or am I in the house with an intruder?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Sheldon, I got to go inside. It&#8217;s getting rough out here.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: You&#8217;re dodging the question, I knew it was you. What was that?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: What was what?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: This isn&#8217;t a very good connection, but it sounded like someone just released a kraken.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Okay, I&#8217;m hanging up now. You know there&#8217;s no such thing as a k&#8230; <i>(Leonard is attacked and dragged into the sea by a huge tentacle. Sheldon wakes up in bed.)</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: No!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: Penny&#8217;s apartment door.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: <i>(Knock, knock, knock) </i>Penny. <i>(Knock, knock, knock) </i>Penny. <i>(Knock, knock, knock) </i>Penny. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: What&#8217;s the matter?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Um, well, I was worried that you might be missing Leonard. And that might be causing you to have bad dreams, like the kind you&#8217;d get if you watched Clash of the Titans right before you went to bed.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Sweetie, did you have a bad dream?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: To be honest, I did.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Aw.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Back to the Future II was in the Back to the Future III case. Leonard did it.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Good night.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: No, wait. Perhaps I should sleep here so you don&#8217;t miss Leonard as much, uh, &#8217;cause you&#8217;re being kind of a baby about it.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: You know what? That would make me feel better. Thank you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: You&#8217;re welcome. Good night.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Credits sequence.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The cafeteria. </i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: I think you&#8217;d be pleased to hear that this morning in the parking garage I saw this oil stain on the ground that was shaped just like my ex-girlfriend, Lucy, and I didn&#8217;t get upset at all.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: I&#8217;m proud of you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Well, you should be, &#8217;cause she was looking good.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Dear Lord, you&#8217;re an astronomer. Although you may have earthly woes, get your mind back on the stars. Gee, even the lowly dung beetle chooses to plot its course by using the Milky Way.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Is that true?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Everything I say is true. Now, of course, the dung beetle also enjoys eating faeces, living in faeces and making little balls out of faeces, so, you know, pick and choose which aspects of its lifestyle you want to embrace.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Come on, you can talk to girls now. It shouldn&#8217;t be hard for you to meet someone new.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: How can I meet someone new when everywhere I look, I see Lucy&#8217;s face? Tell me you don&#8217;t see her smile in the crust of this chicken pot pie.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Oh, will you stop it. Now, listen to me, there&#8217;s a welcome party for incoming post-docs tonight. Go to it and meet someone who isn&#8217;t made of grease or pie.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: You think you&#8217;re so cool because your wife is a person?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Look, Bernie&#8217;s at a neuroscience conference with Amy, I&#8217;ll go with you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: You would do that for me?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Of course. You&#8217;re my friend. I want you to be happy.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Thanks. Oh, Sheldon, since Amy&#8217;s out of town, would you like to join us?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: I want you to be happy, too, but not enough to do anything about it.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: A hotel room.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette<i> (on phone)</i>: Howie, stop. I can&#8217;t talk like that. Amy&#8217;s right here.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy<i> (on phone)</i>: Sheldon, stop. For the last time, I will not bring home bed bugs. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: The hotel&#8217;s nice. There&#8217;s a pool, a gym, the bar looks like fun. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Because I looked in the bed, and there are no bugs.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Aw, I love you, too. If I don&#8217;t talk to you before you go to sleep, I&#8217;ll meet you in dreamland.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Good night. No, I will not consider sleeping in my garment bag.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The apartment.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Penny, did you ever wonder how Starfleet captains&#8230;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: No.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Well, now that I&#8217;ve piqued your interest, welcome to the exciting world of 3D chess.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Why don&#8217;t you just admit you only want to play this game because you always play it with Leonard and you miss him?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: You overestimate his significance in my life.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Mmm.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Do I miss how he makes a face on my toaster waffle with syrup? No. Do I miss the way he fixes the zipper on my jacket when it gets stuck? I don&#8217;t think so. Do I miss how we say good night to each other through the walls of our bedroom using Morse code? <i>(Taps out No in morse code on the table)</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Okay, I get it, I get it. You are an emotionless robot.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Well, I try.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: All right, let&#8217;s just get this stupid game over with.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Great. I&#8217;ll go first.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Okay.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: By the way, how are you with zippers?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Why?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Well, I really need to go to the bathroom, and this one&#8217;s gone all cattywampus.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The mixer party. </i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Wow, your work on jellyfish neural nets sounds so interesting. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Girl: It is. You can download my paper off the university server.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: I will. You can download my paper on the Van Allen Belts from the university server as well.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Girl: I will.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: All right.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: How&#8217;d it go?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Well, if you like dry, factual statements interspersed with painful moments of silence, it was bananas.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Check it out. Mrs. Davis from Human Resources is here. She&#8217;s probably on the lookout for sexual harassment.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Oh, great. There go my chances of being sexually harassed.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: I heard her husband left her for a hot, young undergrad.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: That&#8217;s so much better than the old, ugly ones.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Mrs Davis: Mr. Wolowitz, Dr. Koothrappali. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Mrs. Davis, nice to see you. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: You know, I recently read a fascinating article on infidelity among penguins.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Mrs Davis: Okay.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: So if the fact that your husband left you makes you feel unattractive, just remember, penguins get cheated on, and they&#8217;re adorable.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: It was better when you couldn&#8217;t talk to women.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The hotel bar.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: I was thinking of going to the lecture on posterior cingulate cortex lesions in the formation of autobiographical memory.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Oh, brain lesions are fascinating. Unless they&#8217;re yours, then they&#8217;re a drag.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: To the advancement of science.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: And to the sick and dying who make it possible.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: This is fun, we never really get to talk shop with Penny around. We usually just end up talking about boys.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Which is fine, but it&#8217;s nice to mix it up with a little intellectual conversation.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Waiter: From the two gentlemen at the bar.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Oh, my gosh, boys bought us drinks, boys bought us drinks. Thank you, thank you, thank you so much.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Be cool.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: You be cool. Guys are hitting on us, and not just to get to Penny.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: You&#8217;re right. Thank you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The apartment.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Ooh. Bad move.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Really? Why?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: My queen can now take your rook from below.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: So that means I lose, right? It&#8217;s over?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: If I make this move, but I won&#8217;t because we&#8217;re having too much fun.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Okay, let&#8217;s take a break. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: We&#8217;re all out of alcohol.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: I wasn&#8217;t going to get alcohol. Gosh, I wonder what Leonard&#8217;s doing right now. I miss him so much.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Well, if you&#8217;d like, we could call him. I mean that you could call him. As I&#8217;ve explained, the absence of my friends does not cause me pain. As rock and roll bad boy Paul Simon once said, I am a rock, I am an i-i-i-island.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: I&#8217;m calling him.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, goodie, put him on speaker phone.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard <i>(on board boat, at a party, dancing)</i>: Excuse me, ladies, my pants are buzzing. North Sea, how can I kelp you?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Leonard?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Penny? Hey, it&#8217;s Penny. Everybody say hi to Penny.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Everybody: Hi, Penny!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Wow, it sounds like you&#8217;re having a good time.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Best time of my life. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Isn&#8217;t it five thirty in the morning there?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Is it? Hey, everybody, it&#8217;s five thirty in the morning!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Uh, okay, well, we were just calling you because we were missing you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Random voice: Iceberg!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Uh-oh, hang on.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Are you in danger?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: No, it&#8217;s a drinking game. Whenever we see an iceberg, we take a shot!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Everybody: Berg! Berg! Berg! Berg! Berg! Berg!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Leonard, Leonard? I cannot believe we were missing that jerk.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: You were.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The hotel bar.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: So what happened? How&#8217;d it go?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: It&#8217;s fine. I thanked them again and let them know we&#8217;re not available.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: So I can drink this without having to give up the goodies?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Yes, it&#8217;s all cool. Although, if you wanted to talk to one of them, no one would blame you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: And why would no one blame me?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: I don&#8217;t know what I&#8217;m saying.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Well, it sounds like you&#8217;re saying that I could do better than Sheldon.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Boy, these drinks are strong. Hoo, mama, I&#8217;m gonna be huggin&#8217; the toilet tonight.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: No, tell me, I want to know what you meant by that.</span></p>
