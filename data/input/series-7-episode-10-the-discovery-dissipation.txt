<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: I do see what you&#8217;re saying. That helps.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: No, we were waiting for you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Aw, that&#8217;s so sweet.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Well, you&#8217;re welcome.</span></p>

<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Oh, there is no winning with you. And koalas and otters don&#8217;t even live near each other.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: That&#8217;s what makes their friendship unlikely,</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Hey, what&#8217;s with all the yelling?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Leonard disproved my element. Now all the attention is going to go away.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh, that&#8217;s great. You must be thrilled, </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: That&#8217;s it, I&#8217;m down to seven friends.</span></p>
