<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: Wo de zhing shi Sheldon.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;"><span style="font-family:Calibri;">Howard: No, it’s Wo de ming zi shi Sheldon. <em>(Makes a hand movement with every syllable.)</em></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;"><span style="font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: Wo de ming zi shi Sheldon.<em> (Copies hand movements.)</em></span></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: That’s what you did. I assumed as in a number of languages that the gesture was part of the phrase.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: Well it’s not.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: How am I supposed to know that? As the teacher it’s your obligation to separate your personal idiosyncrasies from the subject matter. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: You know, I’m really glad you decided to learn Mandarin.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: Why?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: Once you’re fluent you’ll have a billion more people to annoy instead of me. </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: My apologies Leonard, I’m only as good as my teacher. </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: If I were you, I’d be more concerned by what they’re passing off as chicken. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny <em>(storming in)</em>: I need to use your window.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Oh, yeah, no, sure, go ahead. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;"><span style="font-family:Calibri;">Penny <em>(opening window)</em>: Hey Jerkface, you forgot your iPod! <em>(Throws it out.)</em></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: What’s going on?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;"><span style="font-family:Calibri;">Penny: Oh, I’ll tell you what’s going on, that stupid self-centred bastard wrote about our sex life in his blog. <em>(Out of window) </em>Drop dead, you stupid self-centred bastard! <em>(To Leonard) </em>Thank you. <em>(exit)</em></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: Okay, where were we? </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: Not now, I have a blog to find. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><em><span style="text-decoration:underline;"><span style="font-size:small;"><span style="font-family:Calibri;">(Credits sequence)</span></span></span></em></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><em><span style="font-size:small;"><span style="font-family:Calibri;">Scene: Leonard approaches Penny’s door and knocks. </span></span></em></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Penny, are you okay?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny <em>(voice off)</em>: I’m fine, Leonard, just go away.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Look, I understand that breaking up with someone can be very painful&#8230;.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: GO AWAY!</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Okay, feel better, bye. <em>(Goes back to apartment) </em>She doesn’t want to talk. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: Not surprising. Penny’s emotional responses originate from the primitive portion of the brain known as the Amygdala, while speech is centred in the much more recently developed Neocortex. The former can easily overpower the latter giving scientific credence to the notion of being rendered speechless. <em>(Leonard stares at him.) </em>Or maybe she just doesn’t want to talk. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Raj <em>(entering)</em>: Hey, look, I found an iPod. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: It’s smashed beyond repair, what are you going to do with it?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Raj: What else? Sell it on ebay as slightly used. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: It was Penny’s boyfriend’s, they broke up.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: Apparently he posted intimate details of their physical relationship on his blog, which I cannot find anywhere. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: You know what, I’m going to go back and try talking to her again.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: Good idea. Sit with her, hold her, comfort her, and if the moment feels right, see if you can cop a feel. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: I’m not going to do that, Howard.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: I’m not aware of any social convention that requires you to intervene at all.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: What about “damsel in distress?”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Sheldon: Twelfth century code of chivalry, not exactly current. You’d also have to be knighted for that to apply. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: I don’t care. She’s upset, I’m going over there. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: Remember to sit on your hands a bit so they’re warm. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: I’m her friend, I’m not going to take advantage of her vulnerability.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Howard: What, so you’re saying that if in the depths of despair she throws herself at you and demands you take her, right there, right now, you’ll just walk away?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: I said I’m her friend. Not her gay friend. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><em><span style="font-size:small;"><span style="font-family:Calibri;">Scene: Penny’s flat. Penny is eating ice cream from the tub. </span></span></em></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard <em>(knocking and entering)</em>: Hey, listen, I know you said that you didn’t want to talk&#8230;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: I don’t. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard <em>(leaving)</em>: Sorry.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: Wait. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Wait, did you say wait?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: Tell me the truth. Am I just an idiot who picks giant losers? </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: Okay, so I pick good guys, but turn them into losers. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Of course not.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: Well, it’s got to be one or the other, which is it?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: I’m sorry, what were the choices again? </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: I really thought Mike was different, I thought he was sensitive and smart. I mean, not you smart, normal non-freaky smart. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Yeah, no, sure.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: You know, and then he just goes and has to humiliate me by writing about me on his blog so the entire world can read it.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Actually it’s not all that easy to find. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Penny: Yeah, really, well my friends at work found it, my sister found it, judging by my email a number of prisoners at the Michigan State Penitentiary found it. </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Okay, well, what exactly did this guy write, not that I need to know the details of your sex life, I just thought&#8230;. never mind. </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Elevators, parks, movie theatres, out of curiosity, is this subway the transportation system or subway the sandwich shop? </span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin:0 0 10pt;"><span style="font-size:small;font-family:Calibri;">Leonard: Huh. Okay. But my point is that you have absolutely no reason to be embarrassed. </span></p>
