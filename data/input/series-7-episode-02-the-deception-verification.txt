				<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The stairwell</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: And here&#8217;s another interesting weather fact.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Another? Great.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Changes in jet streams can affect the speed at which the Earth rotates on its axis, so bad weather can actually make the day longer.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Well, there must be a hell of a storm somewhere.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Joke if you must, but you&#8217;re going to miss these moments. With Leonard home in a few days, this was your last time driving me to the grocery store.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: You know, I will miss this.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: I&#8217;ll tell you what, if my apples are mealy, we&#8217;ll hit the produce section for one last crazy blowout. Heck, you can even push the cart. Please don&#8217;t take my looking forward to Leonard&#8217;s return as criticism of the job you&#8217;ve been doing in his absence.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: I won&#8217;t.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: That criticism will come later in your report card.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Yeah, I didn&#8217;t stay for the detention, I&#8217;m not gonna read the report card.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard <i>(in Penny&#8217;s apartment)</i>: Hello.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny <i>(screams)</i>: Leonard! Hi!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Keep your voice down.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh, my God. You weren&#8217;t supposed to be here till Sunday.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: We finished the experiment early, so I thought I&#8217;d come home and surprise you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh, my gosh, why are we whispering?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: I didn&#8217;t tell Sheldon, so we could have a few days alone.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh, that is so romantic.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Uh, sure, that&#8217;s why I did it.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh, I just cannot believe you&#8217;re here.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon <i>(off)</i>: Penny, it&#8217;s your lucky day. Three of the eggs are clearly not jumbo. Grab your keys.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Credits sequence.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The comic book store.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Uh, Stuart, I was wondering if you could help me find something.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Stuart: Happy to, unless it&#8217;s hope or a reason to live.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, you make me laugh, sad clown. Anyhoo, Leonard will be back in a couple days and I need a welcome home gift for him. As he&#8217;s been at sea, perhaps something with a nautical theme might be appropriate.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Stuart: Okay, well, I don&#8217;t know how much you want to spend, but I do have this pretty cool Aquaman statue.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Aquaman? Oh, this isn&#8217;t a gag gift, Stuart.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Stuart: Yeah, just as well. It&#8217;s a pretty rare piece. I&#8217;d rather just sell it to a real collector.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: I&#8217;m a real collector. How rare is it?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Stuart: Oh, I shouldn&#8217;t even have mentioned it. How about a Batman squirt gun?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Don&#8217;t try and trick me into buying something I don&#8217;t want. Now let&#8217;s talk Aquaman. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: What were they thinking, putting Doctor Octopus&#8217;s mind in Spider-Man&#8217;s body?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Well, I&#8217;ve been quite enjoying that. It combines all the superhero fun of Spider-Man with all the body-switching shenanigans of Freaky Friday. Both versions, original and Lohan.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Both versions: original and Lohan. You&#8217;re an idiot.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Hey, what&#8217;s your problem?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: I&#8217;m sorry, I&#8217;ve been kind of snippy lately. It&#8217;s probably this stupid diet I&#8217;m on.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Why are you on a diet?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: I&#8217;ve put on a couple pounds. Had to buy these pants in the men&#8217;s section.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Raj: Well, we&#8217;ve all seen your mom. That Butterball turkey was bound to come home to roost.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Twelve hundred dollars. That&#8217;s my final offer.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Stuart: All right, Sheldon, you win. I&#8217;m sure Leonard is gonna love this.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, right, a present for Leonard. You better throw in that squirt gun.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Stuart: Ooh, yeah, I don&#8217;t know. This squirt gun, it&#8217;s, uh, it&#8217;s pretty rare.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: Penny&#8217;s apartment. </i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: And this is me doing the Titanic pose on the boat.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Ah.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: And, oh, that&#8217;s me getting rescued after I fell in.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh. <i>(Knock on door) </i>Oh, that&#8217;s the pizza.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: Yep. Here&#8217;s some money.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Thank you.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Leonard: And I&#8217;m gonna hit the head. That&#8217;s what us salty sea dogs say when we have to go pee-pee.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Hey.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Pizza guy: Twenty-two fifty.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Okay, here&#8217;s, uh, twenty-five. Keep the change.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Pizza guy: Seriously? I just walked up, like, four flights of stairs.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh. Okay. Well, here&#8217;s, um, thirty something cents and a promise I won&#8217;t call your boss and tell him you reek of marijuana.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, Penny, I see you&#8217;ve ordered pizza. I have Chinese food. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: That&#8217;s nice.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, that&#8217;s a rather earthy cologne. My uncle used to wear that. Perhaps we can enjoy one last meal together before Leonard returns.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Yeah, thanks, but I kind of feel like eating alone tonight, so&#8230;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Are you sure? With your Italian pizza and my Chinese noodles, we could play Marco Polo. I mean, of course, a re-enactment of a meal in the life of Marco Polo the Venetian explorer, not Marco Polo the terrifying water sport.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Uh, yeah, it sounds fun, but no thanks. <i>(Toilet flushes) </i>Have a good night!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: What, now, do you have company?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: No. No, no, no. You know what? The toilet&#8217;s been doing that. I called the building manager, so&#8230;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, I can take a look at it.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Well&#8230;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: I&#8217;m quite familiar with plumbing. Not to brag, but I spent most of fifth grade with my head in a toilet.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Yeah, no, no, no. You know what, Sheldon, it&#8217;s okay. You don&#8217;t have to go into the bathroom.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: That&#8217;s curious. If there&#8217;s no one here, why are there two glasses of wine on the table?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh. Well, you know, I, I&#8217;ve got two hands and a bit of a drinking problem.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Of course. Ask a silly question. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Oh. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: That&#8217;s odd.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Um, what?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: There are takeout containers in the trash can.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: So? That&#8217;s my dinner from last night.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: What&#8217;s odd is they&#8217;re in the trash can.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: Okay. Look, honey, I promise there&#8217;s no one&#8217;s here. <i>I</i>&#8216;ve had a long day. I just want to have a quiet dinner by myself.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, very well. I&#8217;m no stranger to enjoying the pleasures of solitude. Oh!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: What?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Have you gotten Leonard a welcome-home gift yet?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Penny: No.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, great. Do you want to go halfsies on a two hundred dollar squirt gun?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: Howard and Bernadette&#8217;s apartment.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Hi, honey.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Hey.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: I made some brownies. You want one?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: You&#8217;re kidding, right? I mean, you know I&#8217;m trying to lose weight. God, I thought we were partners in this marriage.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: We are. Stop it. And for the last time, you&#8217;re not fat.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Really? Tell that to the bathroom scale, &#8217;cause one of you is lying.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Fine, forget I asked. How was dinner at your mom&#8217;s?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Awful. I had to rub her ointment all over her again.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Why can&#8217;t she do it?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: &#8216;Cause we&#8217;ve got a deeply unhealthy relationship. Which reminds me, do you think you can get any samples of this from work? This was supposed to last her a month, but they didn&#8217;t take into account the square footage of her back.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Let me see. How long have you been putting this on her?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: I don&#8217;t know. Few weeks. Why?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: This is really strong oestrogen cream. Please tell me you&#8217;ve been wearing gloves.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Like these swollen sausages could fit in gloves.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: Howie, the oestrogen&#8217;s getting absorbed by your skin. That&#8217;s why you&#8217;ve been all bloated and moody and a giant pain in the ass. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: You&#8217;re full of oestrogen and you don&#8217;t act like that.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: That&#8217;s &#8217;cause I&#8217;m a woman. I&#8217;ve had years of practice riding the dragon.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Fine. I&#8217;ll wear gloves next time.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: It&#8217;s still gonna take a few weeks for the hormones to leave your system.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: I feel so stupid. And fat.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Bernadette: It&#8217;s okay. You still look great to me. In fact, why don&#8217;t we go in the bedroom and I&#8217;ll prove it to you?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Howard: Sex? Really? I mean, that&#8217;s just your solution to everything. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: The apartment.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Oh, and here&#8217;s a fun thing. I worked it out so that there are two different words for spoon, planko and janko. Planko is a spoon with food, janko is a spoon without food. Janko is spelled with a silent ptang. Sheldon, you&#8217;re not even listening to the rules of my made-up language.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Yes, I am.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Then what does tweepadock mean?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Uh, elephant?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Lucky guess.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: I&#8217;m sorry. I&#8217;m just distracted by something that happened over at Penny&#8217;s.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: What happened?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: I fear Penny is being unfaithful to Leonard.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: What?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: She claimed to be alone when there was obviously someone else in her apartment. I have no choice but to assume the worst, given Leonard&#8217;s lengthy sea voyage and her famously ravenous nether regions.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: I don&#8217;t think Penny would cheat on Leonard.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Oh, really? She and I once had a staring contest. She clapped really loud and made me blink. It&#8217;s a small leap from there to sexual infidelity.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: You&#8217;re being ridiculous.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: Amy, there were Chinese food containers in the trash can.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Poor Leonard.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;"><i>Scene: Sheldon and Amy listening at Penny&#8217;s door.</i></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Do you hear anything?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: I hear a woman&#8217;s voice.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Is it Penny?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Sheldon: No, it&#8217;s you. All right, I hear whispering and giggling. Now I think I hear kissing.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size:small;">Amy: Yeah, like you know what kissing sounds like.</span></p>
